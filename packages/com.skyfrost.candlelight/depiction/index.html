CandleLight

  • Ever wake your phone and realize that your battery has been sucked up by the power hog that is the flashlight?

    "Oh no! I forgot to turn it off!"

    Face this horror no more, CandleLight turns your flashlight off after a specified amount of time.

    Alternatively, it can be configured to show an alert and even vibrate to let you know that it has been left on.
